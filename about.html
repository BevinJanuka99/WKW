Wijeweera Knit Wear (PVT) Ltd

Sri Lanka's knitwear leader, defined by quality, trust, and innovation.

A Legacy Knited in Quality

For over 69 years, WKW has been at the forefront of the Sri Lankan knitwear industry, specializing in superior quality innerwear, casual wear, and customized corporate apparel. We are committed to ethical manufacturing and exceptional customer value.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, comfortable, and durable knitwear while maintaining sustainable and ethical manufacturing practices.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred knitwear brand and manufacturer in the South Asian region.

Core Value

Commitment to Quality: From raw cotton to the final stitch, we ensure excellence and durability in every garment.
