Our Story: A Thread of Legacy

Tracing two decades of growth, innovation, and commitment to the Sri Lankan garment industry.

Milestones That Defined Us

Innovation and dedication, woven into every step of our journey.

1956: The Founding

Wijeweera Knit Wear is established with a small team and a dedication to high-quality cotton innerwear, laying the foundation for local brand trust.

2005: Brand Expansion

Introduction of our key domestic brands, including **Ballerina** and **Mantex**, quickly becoming household names in Sri Lanka.
