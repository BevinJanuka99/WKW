Gents Trunks - Davi Jones

The modern solution: a snug fit, superior support, and minimal bunching. Perfect for tailored clothing.

Available in standard white, black, and navy blue.

Davi Jones

Tailored fit, maximum mobility.

The Mantex Trunk is designed for the modern man. It features a shorter leg than a boxer brief, providing the necessary support of a brief with better coverage, ensuring a smooth silhouette under slim-fit trousers and jeans.

Key Features:

  • **95% Combed Cotton / 5% Spandex:** The perfect blend for stretch and all-day comfort.
  • **Anti-Ride-Up Leg:** Silicone grips and reinforced cuffs prevent rolling or bunching.
  • **Comfort Waistband:** Soft, branded elastic that won't dig or irritate the skin.
  • **Flatlock Seams:** Minimizes friction points, ideal for sports and long work days.

Gents Trunks Sizing Guide

Trunks are designed to be form-fitting. Choose your size based on your natural waist measurement for an ideal, supportive fit. If you are between sizes, we recommend sizing up.
