Ladies Petticoats - Ballerina

The essential foundation layer for traditional and light-colored garments. Our petticoats provide a smooth, opaque finish.

Available in various colors and lengths (36" to 42").

Ballerina

Traditional Quality for Everyday Use.

The Ritton Petticoat is a core piece of ladies' innerwear. It is designed to be worn under sarees, skirts, and dresses, offering a layer of modesty, preventing clinging, and adding shape to outer garments. Known for its generous cut and robust fabric.

Key Features:

  • **Material:** Durable Cotton/Poly blend for long wear and easy care.
  • **Drawstring Waist:** Fully adjustable for a customizable and comfortable fit.
  • **A-Line Cut:** Provides adequate volume and movement under traditional clothing.
  • **Color Fastness:** High quality dyes ensuring colors remain vibrant after washing.

Ladies Petticoat Sizing Guide

Petticoats are primarily sized by **Hip/Waist Circumference** and **Length**. The drawstring allows for fine adjustment at the waist.
