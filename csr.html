Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

We weave sustainability, ethical labor, and community care into every aspect of our business.

Our Pillars of Responsibility

Environmental Stewardship

  • Waste Reduction: Implementing strict policies for fabric and material waste minimization.
  • Water & Energy: Utilizing modern machinery to reduce water consumption and optimize energy usage in dyeing and finishing.

Ethical Labor Practices

  • Fair Wages & Safety: Strictly adhering to Sri Lankan labor laws, ensuring safe working conditions and competitive compensation.
  • Employee Welfare: Providing health benefits, training programs, and a supportive, inclusive working environment.

Community Support

  • Local Development: Sponsoring educational initiatives and providing donations to local schools and community centers.
  • Disaster Relief: Actively participating in relief efforts during natural calamities in the local region.
